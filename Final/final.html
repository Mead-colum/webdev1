Carlos Eiene "insaneintherainmusic"

I found insaneintherain after his albumSinnohvationwhich was made up of jazz covers of tracks from Pokemon Platinum. insaneintherain mainly did video game covers but recently decided to turn purely to original works with a little "debut" album fittingly called insaneintherain I really do recommend his works

Sonic (music)

Live and learn Live and learn Live and learn, I love this song so much. However I also love most Sonic game music, knight of the wind, escape from the city, let the speed mend it, all of sonic rush ost, etc. I like the games to, when they're good.
